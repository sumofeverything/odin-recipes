Creme Brulee

Description

Crème brûlée is a rich and creamy chilled custard topped with a layer of hardened caramelized brown sugar that cracks when you tap it with a spoon. It's the ultimate decadent dessert to serve when entertaining!

Ingredients

  • Egg yolks
  • White sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Cream
  • Brown sugar

Steps to follow

  1. Whisk the egg yolks, sugar, and vanilla in a bowl.
  2. Heat the cream until it's almost boiling, then whisk the cream into the egg mixture.
  3. Pour the mixture into a double boiler and stir over simmering water until thick.
  4. Pour the mixture into crème brûlée dishes or ramekins.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven until set.
  6. Cool to room temperature, then chill in the fridge.
  7. Sprinkle each custard with a blend of brown and white sugars.
  8. Place under your oven's broiler until the sugar is melted and caramelized.
  9. Chill before serving.
